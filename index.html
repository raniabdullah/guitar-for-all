About Guitar For All

At Guitar For All, our mission is to empower individuals to pursue their passion for learning the guitar, regardless of language barriers. We provide free beginner guitar courses in both English and Urdu, aimed at helping people, especially in Pakistan, where there is a lack of resources for learning guitar in Urdu.

This initiative is designed to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to progress to a basic guitar playing level, fostering a love for music and creativity.
